Overview

The historic city of Sydney, set in the expansive Sydney Harbour,
is a cosmopolitan hub surrounded by beaches on one side and
national parks on every other.

Don't forget to check out all the high-end shopping, museums and
parkspace that makes Sydney a world-class destination from
visitors all over the globe.
